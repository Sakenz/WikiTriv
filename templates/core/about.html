About WikiTriv

Welcome to WikiTriv, your ultimate source for interesting and diverse trivia! Our platform leverages advanced AI technology to scrape and curate fascinating facts and insights from Wikipedia. Whether you're a trivia enthusiast, a student, or just someone curious about the world, WikiTriv provides you with a never-ending stream of knowledge to explore.

What we offer

Here the features that we currently offer:

  • Diverse Topics: From history and science to pop culture and technology, WikiTriv covers a wide range of topics.
  • Regular Updates: Our AI scraper ensures that the trivia is always fresh and up-to-date.
  • user-Friendly Interface: Navigate through trivia easily with our clean and intuitive design.
  • Learn and Share: Enjoy learning new facts and share them with your friends and family.

Our mission is to make learning fun and engaging by bringing the vast repository of knowledge from Wikipedia to your fingertips in an easily digestible format.
